# Logo

The app icon was generated for **Mon PF App** as a restaurant and delivery mark:

- red background to match the app UI color system;
- white fork and spoon to represent restaurant ordering;
- dotted route accent and yellow point to represent delivery tracking;
- no text, so the icon stays readable at small launcher sizes.
